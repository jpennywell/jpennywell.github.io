About the play

Someone can’t get a signal. Someone won’t answer the door. Someone put an elephant on the stairs. Someone’s not ready to talk. Someone hates irrational numbers. Someone told the police. Someone got a message from the traffic light. Someone’s never felt like this before.

In a rapid-fire kaleidoscope of over 55 scenes, we watch a hundred different people deal with what they know...or what they don’t know...or what they think they want to know... Love and Information is a play about what it means to be human, to be in love, and to have relationships when we are in a world of information overload.

Looking for more? Check out the New York Theatre Workshop's 2014 production on YouTube.

What actors need to know

  • Auditions are Friday, May 27 and Saturday, May 28, by appointment only.
  • Email us at john@madtheatre.ca or trevor@madtheatre.ca to book an appointment time.
  • Actors should come prepared with an acting resume and a head-shot. Don't worry if you think your resume isn't very long. And you don't need a professional head-shot.
  • We'll try to double-book audition slots. This means you'll have a partner to act with for your audition.
  • Actors do not need to prepare a reading or other piece for the audition. We'll ask you to arrive early to get a chance to read a scene or two from the play, and then you can give it a test-drive during your audition. We're not looking for something polished -- we want to see what kind of character or scene you can create on the spot.
  • Auditions will run 20 minutes.
  • We will be casting about a dozen actors.

Rehearsal Schedule

  • Rehearsals will begin early in September at Gonzaga High School.
  • We will likely have some table reads or other exploration sessions before September rehearsals begin.
  • Rehearsal times will be discussed with actors at their audition.
